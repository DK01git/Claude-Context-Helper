Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Claude Context Helper ("the Extension") is operated by [Your Company Name]. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What We Collect

  • Google account info — your name, email address, and profile picture, obtained through Google Sign-In. We use this solely to identify your account.
  • Usage counts — the number of messages you send per day, to enforce your plan limits. No message content is stored.
  • Screenshots and page text — sent to Claude for analysis. We do not store, log, or train on these. They are discarded immediately after Claude responds.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not store your conversation history.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not track your browsing activity.
  • We do not use cookies or analytics trackers in the Extension.

3. How Your Data Is Used

  • Your Google identity is used to authenticate you and associate usage counts with your account.
  • Messages and screenshots are forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. Anthropic's own Privacy Policy applies to that processing.

4. Data Retention

Your account record (name, email, tier, daily usage count) is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@yourplatform.com.

5. Security

All communication between the Extension and our servers uses HTTPS. Session tokens are stored in Chrome's local storage on your device and are never transmitted in logs.

6. Changes to This Policy

We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance.

7. Contact
